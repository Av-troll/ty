Our Goal

"Our goal is to educate everyone of all ages in a fun style so that they can use whatever they learn in real life, in any situation, so that they develop extra confidence in general. Learning is not necessarily a chore, burden etc. for young minds if taught through games, entertaining slides and easy to understand language, without unnecessarily extending the chapter. We wish you well throughout your learning journey with us!"

~Head and founder,Baani Bajpai Sheryari



About Us

We were just a few kids who were bored their minds out at home. Few of us had taken up a hobby such as piano, guitar or knitting. Some of us were falling behind in our studies. We searched and searched all day, all night, to find a website that taught both academics and fun stuff. But nada, we found none. So, we decided to make our own organisation and website named the learning hub! And yes, we did it all on our own, no grown-up help(Even the website.)It wasn't easy, but we managed all right in the end.

~Our head and founder,Baani Bajpai Sheryari
